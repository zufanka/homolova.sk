Hi! I'm Ada

I am a freelance data journalist, data journalism teacher, speaker and public spending enthusiast.

On a long term, I am working with Follow The Money‘s Bureau Brussel as a data journalist and data coordinator in collaborative reporting. I am also a part of Arena for Journalism in Europe, among others coordinating the data skills track for the European Investigative Journalism Conference Dataharvest.

As guest lecturer I teach Python at the Master of Investigative Journalism at the University of Gothenburg. I am also a proud member of the Open Spending Coalition, coalition for open, fair, and accountable public spending in the EU.
